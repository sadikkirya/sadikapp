    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where is my order? ▼
    You can track your order in real-time by going to the 'Track Order' screen from the home page or your active order widget.
    How do I contact the rider? ▼
    Once your order is picked up, a 'Call Rider' button will appear on the tracking screen. You can also send them a message directly.
    Can I cancel my order? ▼
    Orders can only be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing them. After that, the restaurant may have already started preparing your food.
    My food arrived cold/damaged. ▼
    We are sorry to hear that! Please contact our support team immediately using the button below, and we will resolve this for you.
    What payment methods do you accept? ▼
    We accept Cash on Delivery, MTN Mobile Money, and Airtel Money.
